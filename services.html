Services

Wildlife removal, handled.

From bats in the attic to beavers flooding a roadway, we handle the full range of wildlife conflicts on the North Shore — humanely, durably, and on call 24/7.

01

Bats

Attic · Roofline · Soffit

Bats in attic spaces are a serious health and odor issue. We perform full inspections, install one-way bat valves, seal all gaps, and offer guano cleanup — all in compliance with Massachusetts bat protection regulations and seasonal exclusion windows.

02

Raccoons

Attic · Chimney · Deck

Raccoons cause significant structural damage and carry real health risks. We provide humane removal, locate and secure all access points, and clean up affected areas so your home is safe and properly sealed against future intrusion.

Schedule help →
03

Squirrels

Attic · Soffit · Roofline

Squirrels chew wiring, destroy insulation, and keep families up at night. We find every entry point, install one-way doors where needed, and seal gaps with durable materials so they can't get back in.

Schedule help →
04

Skunks

Deck · Shed · Foundation

Skunks burrowing under decks, sheds, and foundations create unbearable odor and ongoing health concerns. We locate burrows, safely remove the animals, and install exclusion barriers to prevent future digging.

Schedule help →
05

Mice & Rats

Walls · Kitchen · Garage

Rodents spread disease and contaminate food, wiring, and insulation. We combine trapping, exclusion work, and sanitation recommendations to stop current activity and prevent new infestations — without relying solely on poison.

Schedule help →
06

Groundhogs

Decks · Foundations · Yards

Groundhogs undermine decks, sheds, pools, retaining walls, and foundations. Our specialized underground treatment approach humanely clears active burrows, with follow-up exclusion work to keep them from coming back.

07

Beavers

HOA · Municipal · Commercial

Beaver dams flood roads, yards, and critical infrastructure. We provide active management — trapping, flow devices, and ongoing monitoring — tailored for municipalities, HOAs, conservation commissions, and large properties.

08

Opossums

Decks · Sheds · Garages

Opossums often den under decks and sheds or wander into garages and outbuildings. We remove them safely and secure problem areas to prevent future access.

09

Moles

Moles create tunnels and surface mounds that destroy lawns and root systems. Our targeted mole control programs reduce activity and help restore the integrity of your yard.
