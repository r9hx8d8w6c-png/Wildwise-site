About

The North Shore's wildlife specialists.

Built around a simple idea: solve wildlife problems the right way the first time, with humane methods and exclusion work that actually keeps animals out.

WildWise Management was founded to fix a problem most homeowners on the North Shore know all too well — wildlife issues that get "treated" but never really solved. Animals trapped and removed, but no exclusion. Foam shoved into a gap that a squirrel chews through in a week. Poison left in attics where it kills the rodent and creates a far worse smell.

We do it differently. As a licensed Massachusetts Problem Animal Control agent, our work centers on humane removal paired with durable, properly-installed exclusion — heavy-gauge hardware cloth, stainless steel mesh, sealed flashing, and one-way doors timed correctly for the species and the season.

Based in Wakefield, we respond 24/7 across Boston's North Shore — handling everything from bats in attics and squirrels in soffits to raccoons, skunks, groundhogs, and beavers threatening homes, businesses, HOAs, and municipal infrastructure.

Every property is different. Every animal is different. Our job is to figure out exactly what you're dealing with, do the work without shortcuts, and leave you with a property that's sealed against future intrusion. That's it.
